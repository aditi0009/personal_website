ABOUT ME

I am a 4th year student at IISER KOLKATA, pursuing BS-MS integrated degree in physics

Research Interests

My Current research Interests include investigation of Galaxy evolution, The Intra-Cluster Medium between Galaxy Clusters, Space Plasma and related areas in Astrophysics.
In the coming years, I would like to dive deeper into understanding the various aspects of galaxies, for example- I find Active Galactic Nuclei to be quite intriguing and would like to study it. Ofcourse mapping the cosmic Microwave Background Radiation in the upcoming years with better instruments to understand cosmology and astrophysics is also something I would liek to do.

EDUCATION

I am majoring in physical sciences while also crediting courses in Computer Sciences in order to acquire skills for my research.
